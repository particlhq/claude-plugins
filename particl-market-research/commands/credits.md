# /particl:credits

Check your Particl credit balance and see cost estimates for common research tasks.

## Steps

1. Call `get_credit_balance` to get current balance
2. Display remaining credits and usage summary
3. Show cost estimates for common operations:
   - Company search: FREE
   - Company details: 1 credit
   - Market pricing analysis: 1 credit
   - Product queries: 1 credit per row (controlled by page_size)
